Faster Than Light - Justin's website

A planet is being terrorized by giant alien spiders! Do you go down with your crew to save them, or do you leave the planet to die? A pirate ship is stuck to an asteroid, do you try to get them out or simply destroy their ship and scrap what remains? These are all choices you must make while playing Faster Than Light. Faster Than Light, or FTL, is a real time strategy (RTS) space game made in a 2D pixel art style, released by the Indie group Subset Games in 2012. In Faster Than Light, you take command of a Federation ship and must navigate through the dangers of space while making critical decisions that determine the fate of your crew and the galaxy.
